Cybernetherworld


This neural network text generator was trained on the works of Symbolists, Surrealists, Dadaists, Buddhists, ecologists, and sci-fi authors. It is based on GPT-2 and Adam King’s Inferkit model and will continue generating text for any input with florid language describing things such as haunted shipwrecks, alluding to Greek mythology, or seeming to address the nature of its own cybernetic writing if you provide a prompt about art and automatons. Enjoy.
